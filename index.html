About

San Kwon is a writer and critic based in Saigon, Vietnam, where he contributes to the digital publication Saigoneer. In 2025, he graduated from Brown University with a BA in Comparative Literature and Science, Technology, Society (STS). His honors thesis, on the problematic of reading and ellipses, was awarded the Rosalie Colie Prize for best thesis in Comparative Literature, an excerpt of which also received the Preston Gurney Prize for Cultural Criticism.
