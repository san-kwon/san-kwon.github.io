About

San Kwon is a writer and critic based in Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam, where he contributes to the digital publication Saigoneer. In 2025, he graduated from Brown University with a BA in Comparative Literature and Science, Technology, Society (STS), where he received the Rosalie Colie Prize and the Preston Gurney Prize for Cultural Criticism. He can be reached by email.
