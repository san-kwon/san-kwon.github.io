About

San Kwon is a writer and critic based in Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam, where he contributes to the digital publication Saigoneer. In 2025, he graduated from Brown University with a BA in Comparative Literature and Science, Technology, Society, where his honors thesis on the problematic of reading and ellipses received the Rosalie Colie Prize, an excerpt of which also received the Preston Gurney Prize for Cultural Criticism.
